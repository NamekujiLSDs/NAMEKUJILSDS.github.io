List of Mods

N-COMP

This mod is based on orange and black.
It's not too quirky, and I think it makes it easier to see your enemies.


Slug.exe

It is designed to be played comfortably even with low specs by cutting down on capacity, etc. to the limit.
The standard version comes with a unique CSS, but the No_CSS version does not.


NamekujiCompLite

It is as lightweight as slug.exe, but a bit darker in color.


NamekujiMod

It is made up of black, orange and gray.
Some people find it a little hard to see.
I guess there are two sides to the story.


AizenMod_v2

This is a remixed, modified and added version of a mod created by Aizen(愛染明王), a verified comp player from Japan.


NamekujiModv2

It uses textures that look like a game from a long time ago.
Visibility is poor, but it is recommended for those who want to experience a sense of nostalgia.
